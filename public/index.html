Welcome to the 2024 Wolfpack.

Hello!

This is a homebase for the BHSc (Hons) McMaster University Class of 2024 cohort. Your friendly local web developer has revamped the old website to make it easier to navigate, color-customizable to your house, and with more neat pages and whatnot. All of the content here is curated and published by the BHSS Second Year Council.

Go ahead and pick your house in the dropdown below to see the color change! If you want to learn more about houses, head to the spirit page.

Resources

Now that you've selected your house, try exploring the resources, spirit activities, and social platforms that the SYC has made available.

New Initiatives

This website remains a place where you can check for academic updates or spirit announcements, but new initiatives have also been added, such as the ability to login with your McMaster account to access private cohort-specific resources, and a new blog page. If you have feedback or suggestions for this site, please reach out to anyone on the BHSS Second Year Council.
